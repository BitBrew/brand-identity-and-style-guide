1. Logo

The BitBrew logo is a gemoetric perspective of a lowercase block "b," which also creates a bold, abstract geometric shape. The design captures and embodies the characteristics we most want to be known for as a company: boldness, limitlessness, functionality, and agility. Its purpose is to help establish our company as an immediately recognizable and respected member of the high tech community. The vertical lockup is intended for collateral where space is not restricted, such as t-shirts, letterhead, and signage. The horizontal lockup is suitable for webpage headers and navigation, so that the mark can take up the full height of the image space.

The logo is also shown as a simple mark to demonstrate that it can be used without accompanying wording where appropriate. Recommended usage is anywhere that BitBrew is already known, or where intrigue is most beneficial, including a favicon, an app icon, a webpage loading screen, etc.

2. Logo Construction

This design was one of several considered for the BitBrew logo, and was selected because of its striking contrast, simplicity, and visual versatility: the positive and negative spaces change its appearance depending on what you focus on.

The Mark

The BitBrew mark was built to be a shape comprised of smaller, identical triangles.

The repetition of the smaller shapes gives a feeling of order and method, while the asymmetry of the sum shape is bold and arresting.

3. Logo Usage

The brand identity includes a monochromatic black and white color scheme with a green spot color of Hex value #02D298.

In all contexts, the logo mark will either appear in black or green–never in white– while the logotype may appear in either black or white, but never green.

4. Typography

Typograhy for the BitBrew brand comprises two fonts: Industry for the logotype, main titles, and subtitles, and Roboto for subheaders. The heavy, square, uppercase letters of the Industry font lend a solid presence to the logo, while Roboto was chosen for its readability and modern appearance.

As the chosen font for main titles, Industry will always appear in uppercase. There are two weights, including a lighter alternative for situations where the heavy weight would be innappropriately bold.

In contrast, Roboto is not intended for main titles or headers, and should never in all caps. This typography is suitable for paragraphs of text and subheaders.

9. Voice

The BitBrew company voice conveys in writing what our logo and brand identity convey visually. Our voice is direct, knowledgeable, simple, and approachable.

Our customers are intelligent, sophisticated developers and product people, and we want our communication to take place at their level of knowledge. We also want to be as straightforward and uncomplicated as possible: clearly defining what our company offers, and skillfully highlighting how and why it benefits companies.

Direct but courteous.

We say...

Thanks for your feedback. That feature is not currently on our roadmap, but we'll remember it as we plan future releases.

We don't say...

Sorry, that feature is not a priority right now.
